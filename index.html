Welcome to DevOps Learning

Explore the world of DevOps and enhance your skills for efficient software development and operations.

DevOps is a methodology in the software development and IT industry. Used as a set of practices and tools, DevOps integrates and automates the work of software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops) as a means for improving and shortening the systems development life cycle.

Git , Jenkins , Ansible ,Terraform, Dockers ,Kubernetes are some of the tools used in Devops.
